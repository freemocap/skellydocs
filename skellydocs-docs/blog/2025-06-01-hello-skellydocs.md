Welcome to the skellydocs blog! This is where we'll share updates about the skellydocs theme, new components, and tips for building great documentation sites.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why skellydocs?

Every FreeMoCap sub-project needs documentation, and maintaining separate themes across multiple repos is a pain. skellydocs solves this by providing a shared Docusaurus theme that all projects can use.

Write your content. We handle the rest.
